Title:
Questionnaire on Client Feedback and Approval Lifecycle and Its Impact on Project Delivery and Stakeholder Satisfaction
Purpose of the Study:

This questionnaire is part of an academic research study conducted to analyze the effectiveness of client feedback and approval processes and their impact on project delivery timelines and stakeholder satisfaction. All responses will be kept confidential and used strictly for academic purposes.
